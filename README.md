Privacy Policy for Wine Nerd
Effective Date: January 21, 2026

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

Introduction
Wine Nerd ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect
1. Information You Provide to Us
Account Information:

•
Name: We collect your name when you sign in with Apple.
•
Email Address: We collect your email address for authentication and account management purposes.
Optional Information:

•
Wine Academy Code: You may optionally provide a wine academy group code to access premium features.
2. Information We Collect Automatically
User Identifier:

•
We generate and store a unique user ID to maintain your account and save your progress.
Location Information:

•
Coarse Location: With your permission, we collect your approximate location (accuracy of ~100 meters) to show you nearby wine academies on a map. This location data is used only temporarily and is not stored permanently.
Game Activity:

•
Product Interaction: We store your game progress, including wine guesses, statistics, streak data, and completed challenges. This data is linked to your account to provide core app functionality.
Purchase Information:

•
We collect information about your subscription status to provide access to premium features.
How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect solely for App Functionality:

•
Authentication: To create and maintain your account using Sign in with Apple
•
Game Progress: To save your guesses, statistics, streaks, and challenge history
•
Premium Features: To manage your subscription and access to Study Mode
•
Find Wine Academies: To show nearby wine groups on a map when you search for academies
•
Improve the App: To understand how the app is used and fix bugs
How We Share Your Information
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

The only data sharing that occurs is:

•
Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication and data storage. Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
•
Apple: When you use Sign in with Apple, your authentication is handled by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
•
StoreKit: Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We receive only subscription status information, not payment details.
Data Storage and Security
•
Your data is stored securely using Firebase Firestore with industry-standard encryption
•
Authentication is handled by Apple using Sign in with Apple
•
We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
•
Location data is used only temporarily and is not permanently stored
Your Rights and Choices
Access and Control:

•
You can view your statistics and game history within the app
•
You can unenroll from wine academies at any time through the app
Location Services:

•
You can deny or revoke location permission at any time through your device settings
•
The app will still function without location access, but you won't see nearby academies on the map
Delete Your Data:

•
You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at peter@winenerd.app
Subscription Management:

•
You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
Children's Privacy
Wine Nerd is intended for users aged 21 and older (or the legal drinking age in your jurisdiction). We verify age during onboarding and do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the legal drinking age.

Data Retention
•
Account Data: Retained while your account is active
•
Game Progress: Retained to provide continuous service
•
Location Data: Not stored; used only during active map sessions
•
Deleted Accounts: All data associated with deleted accounts is permanently removed within 30 days
Tracking and Analytics
We do not track you.

We do not:

•
Share your data with advertising networks
•
Use your data for targeted advertising
•
Share your data with data brokers
•
Track your activity across other apps or websites
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

International Users
Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers (Firebase) are located. By using Wine Nerd, you consent to this transfer.

Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

Email: peter@winenerd.app

Website: https://winenerd.app
