# Privacy Policy for Wine Nerd

**Last updated: January 2025**

## Overview
Wine Nerd is a wine trivia game. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Data We May Collect

### Information You Provide
- Account information (email, username) if you create an account
- Game preferences and settings

### Automatically Collected Information
- Game progress, scores, and statistics
- Device information (device type, operating system)
- App usage data and analytics
- Crash reports and performance data

### How We Use Your Data
- To provide and improve the game experience
- To save your progress and preferences
- To analyze app performance and fix issues
- To communicate updates or changes
- To personalize content and recommendations
- To display relevant advertisements

## Data Storage
Data may be stored locally on your device and/or on secure cloud servers (Firebase/Google Cloud).

## Third-Party Services
Wine Nerd uses Firebase (Google) for data storage and analytics. Their data practices are described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Sharing and Advertising
We may share your information with:
- Service providers who help us operate the app
- Analytics partners to improve our services
- Advertising partners to display relevant ads within the app
- Third-party partners for marketing and advertising purposes
- Business partners who may offer products or services relevant to you

We may use your data to personalize ads shown to you based on your interests, usage patterns, and demographics.

We may also share or sell anonymized, aggregated, or de-identified data that cannot reasonably be used to identify you.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings or by contacting us.

## Your Rights
You may request to view, update, or delete your data by contacting us. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws like GDPR or CCPA.

## Children's Privacy
Wine Nerd is rated 13+ and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
